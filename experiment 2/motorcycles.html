Interceptor

About Royal Enfield Interceptor

The Royal Enfield Interceptor is a modern classic motorcycle manufactured by Royal Enfield, blending retro design with contemporary performance and technology.

Retro Design: The Interceptor features classic styling cues such as a teardrop fuel tank, sculpted lines, and chrome accents, paying homage to the original Interceptor models from the 1960s.

Engine: Powered by a 650cc, air-cooled, parallel-twin engine, the Interceptor delivers smooth power delivery and a distinctive exhaust note, suitable for both city commuting and highway cruising.

Performance: With around 47 horsepower and 52 Nm of torque, the Interceptor offers ample power for various riding scenarios, praised for its accessible performance throughout the rev range.

Modern Technology: Despite its classic appearance, the Interceptor comes equipped with modern features such as fuel injection, disc brakes with ABS, and optional accessories like heated grips and a flyscreen.

Classic 350

About Royal Enfield Classic 350

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 is an iconic motorcycle known for its vintage design and thumping exhaust note.

Vintage Design: The Classic 350 carries a retro design inspired by motorcycles from the 1950s, featuring a classic fuel tank, round headlamp, chrome accents, and teardrop-shaped toolbox.

Engine: Powered by a single-cylinder, air-cooled, four-stroke, 346cc engine, the Classic 350 delivers a distinctive thumping exhaust note, which is a trademark of Royal Enfield motorcycles.

Performance: While not focused on high-speed performance, the Classic 350 offers decent torque and power for comfortable cruising on highways and city roads, emphasizing a relaxed riding experience over outright speed.

Features: Equipped with basic features such as electric start, disc brakes, telescopic front forks, and twin shock absorbers at the rear.

Himalayan

About Royal Enfield Himalayan

The Royal Enfield Himalayan is a versatile adventure-touring motorcycle designed for adventure touring.

Purpose-built for Adventure: The Himalayan offers riders the ability to tackle a variety of terrain, including rough roads, highways, and off-road trails.

Robust Design: It features a rugged and durable design with a long-travel suspension, high ground clearance, and spoked wheels.

Reliable Performance: Powered by a single-cylinder, air-cooled engine with a displacement of around 411cc, the Himalayan delivers adequate power and torque for both on-road and off-road riding scenarios.
